Dr. Prafful Vishwanath Jatale

Dr Prafful Jatale MBBS, DRM (BARC), DNB (TATA memorial Hospital) , F. European board of Nuclear Medicine , CBNC (USA) , MICNM (New Delhi) , CCMTD. Post Graduation in Nuclear Medicine from RMC, BARC DNB Nuclear Medicine from TATA MEMORIAL HOSPITAL and Senior Consultant and Head , Dept of Nuclear Medicine and PETCT scan

Dr Prafful Jatale is a renowned Nuclear Medicine Physician & Head, Department of Nuclear Medicine and PETCT scan in CARE CIIGMA HOSPITAL since 2013. Dr Prafful Jatale has earned the respect and adulation of many in the medical field with his valuable contributions to the field of Nuclear Medicine. He is the first Nuclear Medicine and PETCT Phsyician in the Marathwada region (Since 2013) . He has performed and Reported many Thousands Nuclear Medicine / PECT Scan.

He is amongst the very few Nuclear Medicine Physician in India to receive Eurpoean Board and CBNC (USA) fellowship. He has published many papers in National & International journals (Under Guidance of Teachers Like Dr Sandip Basu , Dr Venkatesh Rangarajan, Dr Arch Agarwal, Dr Unmesh Takalkar ) and has been invited as a faculty for guest lectures in National and International conferences. 

Professional Achievements & Recognition
